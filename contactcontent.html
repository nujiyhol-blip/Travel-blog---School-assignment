ATMOS CLUB

Trip Advisor Rating: ★★★★

This is at the heart of District One, overlooking the Saigon River. Opt for this hotel for access to your favourite food chains and local cuisine in under five minutes by foot!

The hotel was built in 1930 so you can bask in its European colonial luxury at every turn. Its facilities include a pool, gym, and salon.

Yi Jun's tip: Take advantage of the free breakfast buffet that comes with every booking! The spread is made by seasoned, local chefs.
