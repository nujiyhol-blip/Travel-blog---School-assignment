Accomodations

Hotel Grand Saigon

Trip Advisor Rating: ★★★★★

This is at the heart of District One, overlooking the Saigon River. Opt for this hotel for access to your favourite food chains and local cuisine in under five minutes by foot!

The hotel was built in 1930 so you can bask in its European colonial luxury at every turn. Its facilities include a pool, gym, and salon.

Yi Jun's tip: Take advantage of the free breakfast buffet that comes with every booking! The spread is made by seasoned, local chefs.

Unique Experiences

Elisa Floating Restaurant

Trip Advisor Rating: ★★★★

This ship hosts a Vietnamese restaurant specialising in seafood. Despite its reasonable food prices, luxurious experiences await. Elisa tours the Saigon River for three hours at a time, while offering you a buffet spread and full bar.

Yi Jun's tip: Local acoustic bands grace the stage sometimes, try calling Elisa to check for their presence before visits!

Night Life

ATMOS CLUB

Trip Advisor Rating: ★★★★

Known as the leader in the nightlife industry, this club pulls its weight by providing unique performances nightly.

Its interior is exciting, with two industrial airplane fans as its crowning glory, backlit by strobe lights. Smoke machines are also a plenty.

Yi Jun's tip: Order the snack platters for a fun refresher! They come in stacks of high-tea-like sets.
